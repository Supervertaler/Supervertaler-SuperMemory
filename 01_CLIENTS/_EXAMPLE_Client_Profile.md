# Acme Corporation

## Overview
Acme Corporation is a multinational manufacturing company. Translation work
primarily involves marketing collateral and legal contracts.

## Language preferences
- **Register:** Formal, corporate tone. No contractions in Dutch.
- **Audience:** B2B, senior management level.
- **Brand voice:** Conservative, authoritative. Avoid trendy language.

## Terminology decisions
- "sustainability" → "duurzaamheid" (NOT "bestendigheid")
- "compliance" → "naleving" (NOT "conformiteit")
- "stakeholder" → preserve as "stakeholder" (established loanword)
- See also: [[Acme_Terminology]]

## Style rules
- Use formal "u" form, never "je/jij"
- Dates: DD-MM-YYYY format
- Numbers: Belgian Dutch convention (comma decimal, period thousands)
- See also: [[Acme_Style_Guide]]
