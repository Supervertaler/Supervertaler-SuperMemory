# compliance → naleving

## Preferred translation
**naleving** (nl-BE)

## Rejected alternatives
| Alternative | Reason for rejection |
|---|---|
| conformiteit | Too narrow; implies product conformity, not regulatory compliance |
| overeenstemming | Too vague; better suited for "conformance" or "agreement" |
| nakoming | Acceptable in contract law but not standard in regulatory context |

## Context and usage
- **Legal/regulatory context:** "naleving" is the standard term in Belgian Dutch legal texts for regulatory compliance (e.g., "naleving van de GDPR").
- **Corporate context:** Used in annual reports, compliance officer titles ("nalevingsfunctionaris"), and policy documents.
- **Compound forms:**
  - "compliance officer" → "nalevingsfunctionaris" OR "compliance officer" (loanword, client-dependent)
  - "compliance report" → "nalevingsrapport"
  - "non-compliance" → "niet-naleving"

## Client-specific overrides
- [[Acme Corporation]]: Always "naleving"; never use the English loanword.
- [[Beta Industries]]: Accepts "compliance" as loanword in informal communications.
